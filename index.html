Located on the corner of Ernest & Campbell St Innisfail, Brothers Leagues Club Innisfail is one of Queensland's premier clubs providing members and guests with an enjoyable and memorable entertainment experience.

Brothers Leagues Club Innisfail boasts over 5000 members and is open 7 days a week. Our Club has expanded over the past 20 years to become one of the top social destinations in the Cassowary Coast area.
